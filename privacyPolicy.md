# Privacy Policy for Misrical

Thank you for using Misrical. We value your privacy and want to ensure that you feel comfortable using our app. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you choose to sign in with Google, we collect your name and email address solely to identify your account and provide cross-device synchronisation of your preferences. You are not required to sign in to use the core features of the app.

### Location Data
Our app accesses your device's precise location (GPS and network-based) to calculate the direction of the Qibla and for prayer-time related features. This data is processed locally on your device to provide these real-time features.

If location permission has been granted, we also store an approximate location accurate to approximately 10km on our servers. This information is used solely to understand the geographic distribution of our users and to provide region-relevant features. It is never shared with third parties.

### App Preferences and Settings
If you choose to sign in, your app preferences (such as theme, language, and notification settings) are stored on our servers to allow you to restore them on a new device or after reinstalling the app. If you do not sign in, your preferences are stored locally on your device only.

### Notifications and Alarms
To provide you with timely reminders, our app uses exact alarms and notification services. These settings are stored locally on your device and, if you are signed in, backed up to our servers as described above.

### Device Information
We collect certain technical information about your device to help us improve the app and respond to support requests. This includes your device model, Android version, app version, and device locale. This information is associated with your account and stored on our servers.

## Analytics and Crash Reporting
Our app uses third-party services, including Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Firebase Performance Monitoring, to help us improve the app's stability and user experience. These services may collect non-personal information such as:
- Device information (model, OS version)
- App usage statistics and interactions
- Crash reports and performance data

The data collected is used solely for the purpose of improving Misrical and is handled according to Google's and Firebase's privacy policies.

## Disclosure of Information
We do not share, sell, or disclose any personal information about our users to any third parties. Data stored on our servers is used internally only to provide and improve the application.

## Data Retention and Deletion
You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the address below. Upon receiving a deletion request, we will remove your personal information from our servers within 30 days.

## Changes to this Policy
We reserve the right to make changes to this privacy policy at any time. Any updates will be reflected in the policy linked within the application and on our official distribution channels.

## Contact Us
If you have any questions about our privacy policy or our practices related to Misrical, please contact us at support@misrical.com.
